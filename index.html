Hi!

Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented the internet (or the world wide web) in 1989 while at CERN, the European particle physics laboratory. he is director of the world wide web consortium that he founded and a professor in the Computer Science Department at the university of Oxford. In 2001, He became a fellow of the Royal Society and in 2004 he was knighted.

In 1994 Berners-Lee founded the world wide web consotorium (W3C) at the laboratory of the Computer Science (LCS) at the Massachusetts institue of Technology in Boston. It was made up of Companies that wanted to work together to create Web Standards and also improve the Web. it was quickly agreed that the technology should be free of royalities and patients. Berners-Lee has served as director of this W3C since it was founded. In 1995 Microsoft produced first version of the Internet Explorer and this commercial browser created enormus growth in websites and web users. The web was first used for e-commerce in 1997
